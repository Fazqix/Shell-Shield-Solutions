Endpoint Management

January 1st, 2023

by: Yared Wondimu

Research Team

What is Endpoint Management?

Endpoint Management is the process of controlling devices on the provided network while protecting their data and maintaining their functionalities. This can be done by installing security applications and deploying security policies on all network devices.

What in an Endpoint?

An endpoint is a device that can connect to your business network.

Some examples include:

  • Laptops
  • Desktops
  • Workstations
  • Phones
  • Tablets
  • Printers
  • Servers
  • Internet of Things (IoT) Devices

Why is Endpoint Management Important?

Endpoint Management is necessary for several reasons because it manages security, compliance, and productivity within the technology environment and by doing so it does improve device performance and reduces costs.

Security:

Cyber attacks like malware, viruses, and data breaches are things that can be a problem, but endpoint management can help ensure that endpoints will be secured and protected against those issues. Enforcing security policies, patching vulnerabilities, and providing real-time threat detection and responses is what endpoint management software can do when detecting and preventing security breaches.

Compliance:

Maintaining certain levels of security and compliance is required within organizations for many industries that have regulations. Devices that are properly configured and meet the necessary compliance requirements are what endpoint management can help ensure.

Asset Management:

Maintaining certain levels of security and compliance is required within organizations for many industries that have regulations. Devices that are properly configured and meet the necessary compliance requirements are what endpoint management can help ensure.

Cost Effective:

Reducing cost associated with device management by automating tasks, reducing manual labor, and optimizing device performance is what endpoint management can do. Lowering operating costs and a bettering the technology experience through endpoint management will create a more secure environment to work in, leading to better productivity results.
